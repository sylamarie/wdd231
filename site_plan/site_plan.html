Daily Planner & Budget Tracker - Site Plan

Site Name

Daily Planner & Budget Tracker

The name represents a tool designed to help users manage both their **time** and **finances** in one convenient platform.

Site Purpose

This website provides an all-in-one solution for organizing daily tasks and keeping track of finances. The main features include:

  • 📅 A Daily Planner for managing schedules & goals.
  • 📋 A To-Do List & Goals task management features.
  • 💰 A Budget Tracker to monitor income and expenses.
  • 💬 A Motivational Quote Generator.

Scenarios

Common questions from users:

  • 📝 How can I organize my daily schedule efficiently?
  • 💵 How do I track my expenses and savings easily?

Color Schema

The website will use a light pink & black color scheme:

  • Light Pink (#FFC0CB) - Used for backgrounds & accents.
  • Black (#000000) - Used for text and contrast.

Typography

The site will use:

  • 🎨 Main Font: Poppins (Headings & Navigation)
  • ✏️ Body Text: Open Sans (For paragraphs & descriptions)
